Why We Conduct a Conflict Check

⚖️

Ethical Obligation

Attorneys are required by professional rules to check for conflicts of interest before agreeing to represent a new client.

🔍

Protect Your Interests

A conflict check ensures our firm has no prior relationship with your tenants or other adverse parties that could affect our ability to represent you.

⏱️

Fast Turnaround

Conflict checks typically take 12–24 hours. Our team will follow up promptly with next steps once the check is complete.

🔒

Your Information Is Safe

The information you provide is used solely for conflict-check purposes and is kept confidential within our firm.

What Happens After You Submit

1️⃣

Form Received

Our team receives your conflict check request and reviews the information you submitted.

2️⃣

Conflict Check Conducted

We search our records for any prior relationships with you, your tenant(s), or other parties identified in your matter.

3️⃣

We Follow Up

A member of our team will contact you to confirm there is no conflict and schedule your consultation.

4️⃣

Engagement Agreement

If no conflict exists and we can assist you, we will send an engagement agreement to begin representation.
